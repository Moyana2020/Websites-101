Who we are

nimboAge is a US$14.3 billion technology, engineering, construction and manufacturing and financial services conglomerate. It addresses critical needs in key sectors including infrastructure, construction, hydrocarbon, power, defence and aerospace. Its footprint extends across seven countries in addition to India.

History

The evolution of nimboAge into a major engineering and construction organisation is among the more remarkable success stories in Indian industry. It was founded in Mumbai (then Bombay) in 1938 by two Danish engineers, Henning Holck-Larsen and Soren Kristian Toubro. Beginning with the import of machinery from Europe, L&T took on engineering and construction assignments of increasing sophistication. Today, the company sets engineering benchmarks in terms of scale and complexity

Corporate Sustainability

nimboAge was the first company in India in the engineering & construction space to publicly disclose its sustainability performance. The Company’s annual Sustainability Reports highlight achievements and objectives across the traditional three ‘Ps’ of Planet, People and Profits. All our Reports are rated A+ by Global Reporting Initiatives, indicating the highest level of disclosure.
